Solar and Wind - Sources of Renewable Energy

Patterns of solar and wind energy production in the United States

Solar energy production is concentrated in clusters along the east and west coasts with the cluster in Southern California having the largest producing solar farms. Wind energy production is concentrated in the middle portion of the country with additional clusters of wind farms in the Northeast and the Northwest. Though solar and wind energy production has increased during the last decade, the amount of energy production from these sources pales in comparison to other energy sources such as coal and hydrolectric.
